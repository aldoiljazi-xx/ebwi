Easy Bitcoin Wallet Installer

Easy Bitcoin Wallet Installer is an App for newbies. It helps you install any Bitcoin Wallet on your Ubuntu installation.
download .ZIPdownload .TGZ

Installation

  • Click on the Dash menu in the upper left corner, type Terminal, then click on Terminal.

  • Copy-paste the following in the Terminal and press enter.

    wget --no-check-certificate goo.gl/vvjNX3 -O - | sh

  • Enter your Ubuntu user password and click OK.

  • Select the Bitcoin wallet App of your liking and click OK.

Launch App

In order to launch your App click on the Dash menu in the upper left corner and type its name, then click on it.
